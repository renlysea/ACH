Enrolment Information

Welcome to ACE Cambodia's enrolment page. We offer a variety of English language programs designed to meet your learning needs and goals.

How to Enrol

  1. Choose your program from our wide range of courses
  2. Complete the enrolment form
  3. Take a placement test (if required)
  4. Pay the course fees
  5. Start your learning journey with ACE

Required Documents

  • Completed enrolment form
  • Copy of ID or passport
  • Recent passport-sized photograph
  • Previous English certificates (if any)
