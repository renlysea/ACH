About ACE Cambodia

Welcome to ACE

The Australian Centre for Education (ACE) is Cambodia's premier English language training institution, offering world-class education programs for students of all ages.

With experienced native-speaking teachers and state-of-the-art facilities, ACE has been helping Cambodian students achieve their English language goals for over two decades.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality English language education that empowers students to achieve their academic and professional goals through innovative teaching methods and comprehensive support.

Our Values

  • Excellence: We strive for the highest standards in education
  • Innovation: We continuously improve our teaching methods
  • Integrity: We maintain honesty and transparency
  • Community: We foster a supportive learning environment
